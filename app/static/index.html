😶 Train Face Model
Train a dedicated LSTM on face landmark data (cols 176–1579) from your recorded gestures. No new recording needed — uses the same gestures.csv.
10100
LSTM (64–64) trained on 1,404 MediaPipe face landmark features.
How it works
Face landmarks (468 points × 3) are already captured alongside body/hand landmarks during gesture recording. This model learns to recognise gestures from facial expression alone — useful for users with asymmetric movement patterns.

Train order: Movement → Face → (Audio) → Fusion
